Mixtura Latina at Gleis Garten

We are part of the great Gleis Garten family, the first Food Hall in Vienna. It’s an innovative space where industrial charm meets warm hospitality, a passion for good food, and craft beer.

At our Latin Kitchen stand, you’ll discover much more than food. You’ll find culture, joy, and that special connection that only Latin cuisine can offer. We invite you to enjoy the authenticity of Mixtura Latina: homemade dishes filled with tradition, freshness, and that Latin touch that makes every bite a unique experience, right here at Gleis Garten.
