3. What data we collect

We may collect and process the following personal data:

  • Contact form: name, email address and message content.
  • Newsletter subscription: first name, last name and email address.
  • Cookies and technical data: browser type, device information, IP address and similar technical data (only if you accept cookies).
  • Maps usage: when viewing embedded maps (Google Maps), certain location and usage data may be transferred to Google.

4. Purpose and legal basis for processing

We process your data for the following purposes and on the stated legal bases:

  • To respond to your inquiries and messages (legal basis: Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR).
  • To send newsletters and promotional updates if you have given consent (legal basis: Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR).
  • To ensure the proper functioning and security of our website (legal basis: Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR).
  • To display maps and improve user experience when you accept relevant cookies (legal basis: consent, Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR).

5. Newsletter

We use Brevo (Sendinblue) to manage and send our newsletters. When you subscribe, your data (first name, last name, email) is securely stored and processed by Brevo. You may withdraw your consent or unsubscribe at any time using the link in any newsletter, or by contacting us at info@mixturalatina.at.

6. Cookies and external services

Our site may use cookies and third-party services. Cookies will only be set after you give consent via the cookie banner.

  • Google Maps (Google Ireland Ltd.): used to display location maps. Google may collect technical and location data according to its policies.
  • Brevo: used for newsletter subscription processing and may set service cookies.

You can change or withdraw your consent at any time using the Cookie Preferences page or link.

7. Data retention

We store personal data only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described here or as required by law. Contact form messages will be deleted once your request is resolved unless there is a legal reason to retain them longer. Newsletter lists are kept until you unsubscribe or request deletion.

8. Your rights

Under the GDPR you have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Request deletion of your personal data (where lawful).
  • Request restriction of processing.
  • Request portability of your data (where applicable).
  • Withdraw consent at any time (where processing is based on consent).
  • File a complaint with the Austrian Data Protection Authority: www.dsb.gv.at.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at info@mixturalatina.at. We will respond without undue delay and in accordance with applicable law.

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact: info@mixturalatina.at.
