Mixtura Latina

Welcome to Mixtura Latina, we bring vibrant flavors from Latin America to the heart of Vienna. From crispy gold Empanadas to comforting Pabellón a classic mix of slow-cooked beef, rice, beans, and sweet plantains plus Taco Tuesdays and satisfying weekend brunch, we deliver bold flavors and warm hospitality in every bite.

Vienna's first Foodhall, Gleisgarten is not just a place for culinary delights. From live music to exhibitions to interactive performances, there is always something to discover and experience.

Our restaurant is part of this innovative culinary destination, bringing the flavors of Latin America to this extraordinary setting. Join us for regular events, live music, and an atmosphere that combines industrial charm with warm hospitality.
