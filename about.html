Who we are

We are a family restaurant born from the desire to share something deeply personal: our homemade recipes and the traditions we grew up with.

From the heart of Vienna we bring to your table the authentic flavors of Latin America, the ones that awaken memories, tell stories, and spread joy with every dish.

What we offer

At Mixtura Latina, we celebrate the diversity of our land with a table full of authentic flavors. From our golden arepas and crispy empanadas to homemade dishes like Pabellón Criollo or the classic Arroz con Pollo, every recipe is made with fresh ingredients and that touch of love that represents us.

If tacos are your thing, don’t miss our Taco Days on Tuesdays and Thursdays, and for those who like to start the weekend with good vibes, we offer a Latin Brunch full of color, joy, and flavor.

Our kitchen takes you on a journey from Panama to Chile, bringing to Vienna a 100% authentic, flavorful, and shareable Latin American experience.

The Mixtura Experience

At Mixtura Latina, we believe that going out to eat is much more than just sitting at a table, it’s about living a moment that sparks joy, connection, and memories.

That’s why every visit becomes an experience full of culture, flavor, and good energy. Here, you don’t just taste a dish, you travel through the colors, aromas, and traditions of Latin America.

We want every guest to feel part of our family: to enjoy the warmth of Latin hospitality, share smiles, and experience the kindness that makes everything taste better.

At Mixtura Latina, we don’t just serve food… we serve moments to celebrate life.
