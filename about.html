Who we are

We are a family restaurant that was born from the desire to share something very personal: our home recipes and the tradition we grew up with. From the heart of Vienna, we bring to your table the authentic flavors of Latin America, the ones that awaken memories, tell stories, and bring joy in every dish.

At Mixtura Latina, we believe that food is much more than just nourishment. It is culture, identity, and a bridge that connects people and emotions. That’s why every arepa, empanada, or homemade dish we serve is prepared with fresh ingredients, without preservatives, and with the same care we use when cooking for our own family.

Here you’ll find more than just a restaurant. It’s a place where Latin hospitality, tradition, and flavor come together to make you feel at home, even if you’re thousands of kilometers away.

What we offer

At Mixtura Latina we celebrate the diversity of our land with a table full of authentic flavors.

From our golden arepas and crispy empanadas to homemade dishes like Pabellón Criollo or the classic Arroz con Pollo, every recipe is made with fresh ingredients and that special care that defines us.

If tacos are your thing, don’t miss our Taco Days on Tuesdays and Thursdays. And for those who want to start the weekend with good vibes, we have a Latin Brunch full of color, joy, and plenty of flavor.

Our kitchen takes you on a journey from Panama to Chile, bringing to Vienna a 100% authentic gastronomic experience, delicious, and made to be shared.

The Mixtura Experience

At Mixtura Latina we believe that going out to eat is much more than just sitting at the table: it is living a moment that awakens joy, connection, and memories.

That’s why each visit becomes an experience full of culture, flavor, and good vibes. Here you don’t just try a dish, you travel through the colors, aromas, and traditions of Latin America.

We want every customer to feel like part of our family: to enjoy the music, share smiles, and discover the Latin hospitality that makes everything taste better.

At Mixtura Latina we don’t just serve food… we serve moments to celebrate life.
